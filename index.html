I'm a Product Manager with a background in UX design, focused on crafting user-centered digital experiences that tackle real-world challenges.

I reside in the intersection of technology, user needs, and business objectives, dedicated to dissecting problems, developing product visions, and rigorously testing assumptions.

My career journey seamlessly blends design and product management. I've worked closely with customers, translating their feedback into pivotal features like Bikemap's much-demanded Pause Mode. My commitment lies in crafting products that drive business value and resonate with users.

With a T-shaped skillset, I've fine-tuned my abilities as a UX Designer at Cheil (Samsung's creative agency), KION Digital Campus, and Bikemap, enhancing user experiences across websites, mobile apps, and B2B software.

Beyond work, my passions span cycling, fitness, technology, gadgets, health, and the love of a good story. Coffee and vinyl records also hold a special place.

I'm at my best when working on projects aligned with my personal interests, making a tangible impact, and contributing to a better world. If you're in search of a pragmatic, user-centric problem-solver who bridges the gap between design and product management, let's connect and tackle challenges head-on.
